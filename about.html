Our Story

Empowering Every Ambition

OppHub is an AI-powered opportunity discovery platform built to help students and professionals unlock their full potential.

Our Mission

At OppHub, our mission is simple: eliminate the frustration of opportunity hunting. Every student deserves access to the best internships, scholarships, hackathons, and jobs — regardless of where they come from.

We combine Gemini AI with intelligent filtering and real-time tracking to build the most personalized opportunity management experience ever created for the next generation of Indian talent.

🎓

Scholarships & Hackathons

Built for India's Youth

OppHub is designed with the Indian student ecosystem in mind. From national hackathons to Silicon Valley internships, we surface opportunities that match your academic background, career interests, and ambitions — all in one place.

We believe opportunity should be democratized. Whether you're from a tier-1 city or a small town, OppHub levels the playing field.
