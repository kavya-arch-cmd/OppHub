Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

🔒 Effective Date: 1 May 2026

📋 Overview

OppHub ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data when you use our platform.

📦 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Profile Data: Academic details, course, location, and career interests you voluntarily provide.
  • Application Documents: Resumes, marksheets, and academic documents uploaded during the application process.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, opportunities viewed, and interactions within the platform to improve AI recommendations.

🎯 How We Use Your Information

  • To personalise AI-powered opportunity recommendations using Gemini AI.
  • To process and track your applications with organisations and institutions.
  • To send automated email notifications when your application status is updated.
  • To improve platform performance, features, and user experience.
  • To communicate important updates, policy changes, or security alerts.

📎 Document Handling

Uploaded documents (resumes, marksheets) are stored securely on our servers and are shared only with the relevant opportunity administrators who review your application. We do not sell or share your documents with third parties.

📧 Email Communications

We use your registered email to send application status updates, onboarding guidance, and platform notifications. You may contact us to request removal from non-essential communications. Transactional emails related to your account cannot be opted out of.

🔐 Data Security

Your password is encrypted using bcrypt. JWT tokens are used for secure, session-based authentication. We implement industry-standard security practices including rate limiting, input sanitization, and HTTPS-enforced connections.

📬 Contact

For privacy-related queries, please contact us at our contact page.
